About

Small language.
Big ambitions.

Êde is a constructed language using Latin characters with diacritics to mark sound changes. It follows SVO sentence structure and features a productive system of prefixes and suffixes for deriving new words from existing roots.

The language is actively developed — new words, grammar rules, and community resources are added regularly. Anyone is welcome to learn, contribute, and use it.
